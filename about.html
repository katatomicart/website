Kat Taylor | Nov 1 1992 |

My story is pretty usual - I've been drawing all my life, being largely self-taught until I got into high school and started to take art classes. I entered college knowing I wanted to create stories and draw as much as I could, so I focused my time into writing and honing my skills as an illustrator and painter. I graduated in 2015 from Eastern Washington University as a Studio Art major with a focus in Digital Painting, and a minor in Visual Communication and Design.
